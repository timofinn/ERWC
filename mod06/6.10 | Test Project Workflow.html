If something goes wrong

"Copy Transcript" isn't showing up +

This usually means your iOS version is older than 17. Check: Settings → General → Software Update. If an update is available, you can start it now and it may finish during class — or flag Mr. Finn and try from a partner's phone. Don't skip this step and hope it works later. You need the transcript workflow running before your real interview.

The transcript looks like nonsense +

Two likely causes: you were too far from the phone, or there was too much background noise. Re-record with the phone closer — ideally flat on the desk between you, not held in someone's hand. Also check that Voice Memos has microphone access turned on in your phone's Settings → Privacy → Microphone.

Why this matters for your real interview +

Your transcript is your primary source for the paper you're writing. If the recording fails, you have nothing to write from. If the transcript doesn't copy correctly, you're left trying to reconstruct a conversation from memory. Get every part of this working today — on a low-stakes practice run — so that when you're sitting across from your actual subject, the only thing you're thinking about is listening.
